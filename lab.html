Computing and Communication Systems Laboratory (CCSL) @EC5037

計算與通訊系統實驗室 電資大樓五樓5037室

The Computing and Communication Systems Laboratory focuses on advanced topics in computer systems, artificial intelligence, and modern communication technologies, with research spanning system-level design, edge and cloud intelligence, networking, and AI-driven applications. We aim to bridge theory and practice through research, teaching, and industry collaboration, and we are always looking for talented, motivated students to join our team.
