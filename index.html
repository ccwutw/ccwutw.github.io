Chien-Cheng Wu

A Ph.D. researcher specializing in Reinforcement Learning, Optimization, and Cyber-Physical Systems, I synthesize advanced computational intelligence across theoretical and practical domains. My expertise encompasses large language models, autonomous vehicle systems, and next-generation communications, strategically integrating these complex computational methodologies with transformative technological solutions. Through systematic integration of deep learning architectures with distributed computing paradigms, I develop adaptive approaches that enhance system autonomy, intelligence, and performance across multi-agent networks. My research particularly focuses on optimizing satellite-terrestrial integrated networks for enhanced connectivity and autonomous system coordination. My professional trajectory demonstrates a unique ability to translate complex theoretical frameworks into scalable technological innovations, driving advancements in artificial intelligence and autonomous systems through multi-dimensional problem-solving and cross-domain technological integration.
