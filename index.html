Chien-Cheng Wu

Aalborg University PhD Fellow

I am a PhD student in the Department of Electronic Systems at Aalborg University conducting research on wireless communication and machine learning technologies. I graduated from the National Chiao-Tung University with a BSc and s MSc Degree in Computer Science. My current projects integrate wireless communication engineering and machine learning. I develop new methodologies based on machine learning in the design of wireless systems. I am also an opensource enthusiast. I have been involved with free software development for local and national level.
